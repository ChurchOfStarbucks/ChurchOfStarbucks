RSS Readers

An RSS Reader is a software program / application that let's you specify which blogs or podcasts you are interested in, and will automatically show you new content created by all the websites you "subscribe" to. think of it like Gmail for blogs & podcasts, where you get an item in your RSS Reader inbox any time a blog or podcast you're interested in creates a new item.
