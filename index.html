Church of Starbucks

"Ecclesia", the Greek word for church, predates Christianity. In ancient Greece it wasn't a religious term, it simply referred to "the community". We're using the mildly humorous phrase "Church of Starbucks" to refer to ours.

•     •     •

Our community is as easy as 1,2,3...

1) The #ChurchOfStarbucks hashtag page let's you see what folks are saying.

2) Send a tweet with the #ChurchOfStarbucks hashtag to add your tweet to the hashtag page.

3) The chat tool link creates a URL that you can share with one or more people so you can meet together in your own personal chat room and talk. Post that URL in a tweet, in a DM just to friends depending on who you're looking to chat with.
