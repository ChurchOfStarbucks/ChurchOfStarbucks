Church of Starbucks

"Ecclesia", the Greek word for church, predates Christianity. In ancient Greece it wasn't a religious term, it simply referred to "the community". We're using the mildly humorous phrase "Church of Starbucks" to refer to ours.

The #ChurchOfStarbucks hashtag page let's you see what other Starbuckians are saying in the Twitterverse.

Send a tweet with the #ChurchOfStarbucks hashtag and it will show up on the #ChurchOfStarbucks hashtag page. Use that page as a forum to meet and talk with others. Tell folks about yourself, and your interests, if you're looking for some new friends. Check out the Twitter profile of the folks that post to it, maybe you'll find new friends yourself. You can also use it to folks know when you're heading over to the chat.

The chat tool link creates a URL that you can share with one or more people so you can meet together in your own personal chat room and talk.
