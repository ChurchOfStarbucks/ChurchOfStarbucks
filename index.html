Church of Starbucks

Just because you can't get to Starbucks doesn't mean you can't have a community experience

Public Community Forum

Did you know that the Greek word for church, "ecclesia", predates Christianity? In the Greek world it meant "community", and wasn't a religious term. We'll use the somewhat humorous phrase "Church of Starbucks" to refer to our concept. Sure, we can talk together online, but we'll also think of Starbucks as our club house... our home base... our physical meeting location... our church.
