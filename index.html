Church of Starbucks

"Ecclesia", the Greek word for church, predates Christianity. In ancient Greece it wasn't a religious term, it simply referred to "the community". We're using the mildly humorous phrase "Church of Starbucks" to refer to ours.

Our current community chat can be found via a link in our pinned tweet.

Add a #ChurchOfStarbucks hashtag in a tweet and let folks know when you're heading over to the chat, or to post to a sort of blog for the group that will help others find you.
