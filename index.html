Church of Starbucks

Did you know the Greek word for church, "ecclesia", predates Christianity? In ancient Greece it wasn't a religious term, it simply referred to "the community". We'll use the humorous phrase "Church of Starbucks" to refer to our community. Sure, we can talk together online virtually, but to ground ourselves in the physical world, to remind us that we also need to occassionally meet together physically in person, we'll think of Starbucks as our club house... our home base... our physical meeting location... our church.
