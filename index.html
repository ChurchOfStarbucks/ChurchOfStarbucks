The Church of Starbucks

           

A light hearted name for a serious idea

The internet has enabled many online communities. What if we wanted to extend these online communities to the physical world? We might want to build club houses, as various groups historically did, like the Elks, Masons, VFW, and Christianity did before that. But today, virtually everyone on earth already has a club house near them... it's called Starbucks.

Did you know that the Greek word for "church", which is "ecclesia", predates Christianity? In the Greek world, it meant "community".

What if we re-promoted local "community" using web tools and Starbucks locations as our club houses? That's what this site plans to encourage, and we'll use the somewhat humorous phrase "The Church of Starbucks" to do so. But make no mistake, this is a serious attempt to build local communities. Sure, we'll show you ways to gather together online, but we are also going to promote meeting together physically. We are going to promote people eating together at local restaurants, and we'll also use Starbucks as our equivalent of local churches... our sort of home base physical meeting locations... our physical club houses.

Talk with someone below if you're interested in starting an affiliated "Church of Starbucks" in your area.
