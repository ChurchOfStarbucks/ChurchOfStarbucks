4 free blogging platforms with RSS

(1) WordPress - One of the most popular blogging platforms. Also, one of the more complex. A good platform once your experienced, but but not the best platform to learn with.

(2) Blogger / blogspot - owned by Google. Easier to use.

(3) Tumblr - Owned by Yahoo. Perhaps the easiest of all to use.

(4) Medium - Created by former Twitter CEO. I recommend this very highly, especially in combination with a Twitter account. If your Twitter is @BobSmith your Medium blog URL can be set up as medium.com/@BobSmith. If you're just starting blogging, I recommend getting a Twitter account), and then immediately setting up an assocated Medium account.

Your RSS URL is automatically medium.com/feed/@BobSmith
