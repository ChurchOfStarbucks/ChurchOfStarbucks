Blogging

Free blogging platforms (with RSS)

(1) WordPress - One of the most popular blogging platforms. Also, one of the more complex. Perhaps a good platform once you're experienced, but not the best platform to learn with.

(2) Blogger / blogspot - owned by Google. Easier to use. Few downsides.

(3) Tumblr - Owned by Yahoo. Perhaps the easiest of all to use. A fine starter blog.

(4) Medium - For complete newbies, I recommend this in combination with a Twitter account very highly. Get yourself a Twitter account first, and then immediately set up an assocated Medium account by identifying with your Twitter account. If your Twitter acount is @BobSmith your Medium blog URL will then be set up as medium.com/@BobSmith, and your RSS URL will automatically medium.com/feed/@BobSmith
