Questions? Check the feed!

This community works closely with twitter.com/webulite and our RSS feed. Can't find a link to something you were looking for? The first place to look is the Twitter/RSS feed.

Getting added to the feed

To eventually get your blog/podcast added to the feed, the first step is to connect your Twitter account to your Discord profile. Then, on your Twitter profile, save your blog/podcast URL into the "website" field. This way, clicking on your nickname in Discord, which shows your Discord profile, will provide a link to your Twitter page, and from there one can get to your blog/podcast, and eventually your RSS feed. That's the first step in being considered for the webulite feed.

Ephemeral conversations

We believe that chat should be ephemeral like real conversations, so our Discord server deletes channel posts after a short period of time (about a day).

On Discord, your Direct Messages are not ephemeral. To overcome this you can use an external site for your DM conversations. Instead of holding your conversations with Discord DM, visit the link below, then pass the URL to the person you want to talk with (in a DM), and have them meet you there.
