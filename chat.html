If it's a community, you'll come & go as you do any other club house. But, if you're using the chat one to one, the issue becomes how do you get alerted to the fact that someone is trying to chat with you. I recommend that you move to chat as an extension of Twitter/Facebook, and pass the URL for the 1-1 chat via a Twitter Direct Message or Facebook Messenger.
