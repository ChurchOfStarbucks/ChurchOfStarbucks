Discord Help

Our Discord server deletes channel posts after a short period of time (currently 48 hours). We believe that chat should be ephemeral like real conversations.

Ok... so your channel conversations will be ephemeral, but on Discord, your Direct Messages are not. To over come this problem we recommend you use a site called hack.chat for your DM conversations. Instead of holding your one to one conversations with Discord DMs... pass the URL of a temporary hack.chat channel to the person you want to talk with, and have them meet you there.

Use this link to enter the channel, then copy and paste the URL into a DM of the person you want to speak with. They can meet you there, and then when the conversations is over and you leave, the conversations will dissappear.
